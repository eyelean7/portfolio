About Me

Life

I am from Chicago. I like penguins. I play the oboe. Sometimes I write poetry. Sometimes I write code. Aren't they more or less the same?

In 2013, I graduated with a Bachelor's of Music Education from Illinois Wesleyan University. Since then I have spent time as a early childhood educator, a private music instructor, and a classroom teacher. I hope that in learning to code I can create tools to help teachers, students, and parents.
